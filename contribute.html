HOW TO CONTRIBUTE

1. Research and data collection:

Share your research findings, data, or observations related to extinct species.

2. Citizen Science Projects:

Participate in projects like iNaturalist, eBird, or Zooniverse, which allow you to contribute observations or data.

3. Species-specific databases:

Contribute to databases like the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) Red List or species-specific databases like the Extinct Species Database..

4. Academic publications:

Publish research papers or articles on extinct species in scientific journals.

5. Online platforms:

Share information on online forums, social media groups, or websites focused on conservation and extinct species.

6. Share traditional knowledge:

If you have traditional knowledge or cultural insights related to extinct species, share them with researchers or conservationists.
